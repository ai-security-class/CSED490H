AI Security (CSED490H)

This course studies practical AI red teaming from fundamentals to modern attack surfaces. We cover victim models such as LLMs, VLAs, and agentic systems, along with attack methods including adversarial examples and jailbreaking.

Instructor

Prof. Sangdon Park

Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) and Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), POSTECH.

His research focuses on machine learning and AI alignment, with practical applications in physical AI, computer security, and cyber-physical systems.
